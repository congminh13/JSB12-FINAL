Infrastructure

The infrastructure at Gia Dinh High School has reached level 1 and was officially recognized by the Department of Education and Ho Chi Minh City in 2020.

  • After many years of construction and development, the school has invested in building more spacious and modern classrooms. This investment ensures that they meet the requirements for teaching and learning. Older classrooms have been repainted and equipped with new furniture.
  • Laboratory facilities are fully equipped, providing the conditions necessary for all students to participate in experiments.
  • Classrooms are equipped with air conditioning, projectors, computers, and large LCD TVs. At the beginning of the school year, 10th-grade students were equipped with projectors for their classrooms to support innovative teaching methods.
  • Information rooms: There are five computer rooms with relatively good network configurations and air conditioning, ensuring that every student has a computer for studying and practice.
  • The library is excellent and contains valuable books. Students from all classes can borrow books to study at home. The library is a gathering place for many outstanding students who come to borrow reference materials, study in groups, and discuss.
  • The school's health room provides good services to students. To protect students' health, the school organized boarding services for students who need it. The boarding rooms are cleaned daily before and after students' breaks. According to students' registrations, there are air-conditioned rooms and fan rooms.
  • Each boarding house has many teachers responsible for exams, attendance, and student management.
  • The school's cafeteria is spacious, cool, and hygienic. Students can purchase food vouchers for breakfast and lunch at the school. Students are not allowed to eat on the side of the road where food safety is not guaranteed, and the school gate is damaged.
