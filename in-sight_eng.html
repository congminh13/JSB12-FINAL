School History

  •     Gia Định High School is located in the northeastern gateway of Ho Chi Minh City, situated at 44 Vo Oanh Street, Ward 25, Binh Thanh District, Ho Chi Minh City.
  •     The school was founded in 1956 and was initially part of the Nguyen Duy Khang Parish, with Reverend Vu Khoa Cu as the Director. After the country's unification on April 30, 1975, the school was nationalized, as per Decision 1051/UBNDTP dated June 10, 1978 (signed by Deputy Chairman Van Dai) establishing Thanh My Tay Secondary School.
  •     In the 1995-1996 academic year, the school officially changed its name to Thanh My Tay Secondary School (as per Decision 2826/QD_UB_NCVX, dated August 29, 1994), and starting from the 1999-2000 academic year, it separated its secondary levels and became Gia Định High School as it is today (according to Decision 4914/QD_UB_VX dated September 1, 1999, signed by Deputy Chairman Pham Phuong Thao and Decision 1862/QD_UB dated May 6, 2002, signed by Deputy Chairman Nguyen Thanh Tai).
  •     From its founding until now, for over 30 years, the school has overcome difficulties and continuously developed. Generations of teachers, managers, and students have contributed and left their mark on this place, nurturing and educating young minds for the future.
  •     Many former students of Thanh My Tay - Gia Định have successfully entered various positions in society, contributing to the country's industrialization, modernization, and economic development.

Mission - Vision - Core Values

Mission:

  •     To create a friendly and safe learning environment for all students, regardless of their background.
  •     To educate and provide conditions for students to actively seek knowledge, express personal opinions, and develop a lifelong passion for learning.
  •     To instill ethical foundations and essential skills in students, preparing them to become valuable citizens in the globalized era.

Vision:

  •     In the period from 2016-2020 and looking ahead to 2025, Gia Định High School aims to create a quality educational environment that prepares students from the eastern gateway area to be the most suitable human resources for the modernization and industrialization of Ho Chi Minh City. The school is a place of trust, safety, empathy, and equal opportunities for all students, regardless of their background, to enrich their knowledge, develop specialized skills, acquire life values, and cultivate soft skills. It lays a solid foundation for their future. The school is also a trusted partner of reputable universities in Vietnam and abroad.

Core Values:

  •     Respect, collaboration, and tolerance to empower every member to confidently showcase their abilities, unleash their creativity, and deliver high-quality results in all educational activities.
  •     Honesty, responsibility, and love to create a friendly and safe environment for every member, serving as the basis for a professional educational environment.
